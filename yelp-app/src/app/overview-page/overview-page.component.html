About

The trip of exploring and visualizing.

  • Our Idea

    When you want to explore various kinds of cuisines in a city, you may have no idea where to start even using the Yelp app or Google map. So we wonder if we could explore the data, find the fun facts and demonstrate them in a well-organized way.

  • Our Goal

    The goal of our visualization is to help you explore the features of 100+ cuisines in seven cities through maps, bar chart, coxcomb and etc.

  • Data Source

    The data for the project is from Yelp dataset which encompasses 1.6M reviews, 61K businesses, 366K users and 500K tips.

  • Technical Tools

    • D3 Data Driven Documents
    • DC.js Charting library on D3
    • Angular as framework
    • Bootstrap library

Number of Restaurants on Different Rating and Cuisine

* We use the Coxcomb to show the number of restaurant distribution of rating and various categories of restaurants. The darker green represents a higher rating comparing to a lighter one. You could not only see the distribution in only one category. Check several boxes from those three categories to see and compare the cuisine at the same time.

* For example: If you want to see the star distribution of American food, sandwiches, fast food and bars at the same time, you could select American food from Regional Type, fast food and sandwiches from Food Type, bars from Restaurant Type. Then you could see the coxcomb graph of this city on your interest field.

* We also set some default filters for you to start. Have fun!


    • Light Map on Opening Restaurants

    * The light map animation shows the opening restaurants at a given hour. Every dot represents a restaurant; therefore, you could get an overall view about the busy time of a city.
